How we use our This is the Day App

This is the Day is a designed to record your intentions for the day ahead ( 3 4 Me) and moments that made you Smile Out Loud.

Our day usually goes something like this(ish!):

  1. Cup of tea in bed
  2. Shower with a cold bit at the end
  3. 2nd cup of tea and breakfast
  4. Wim Hoff breathing exercise
  5. Choose about 3 intentions (3 4 for Me)

    3 4 Me suggestions:

    • Have a relaxing morning
    • Join the bees for a coffee in the garden
    • Do some Yoga Nidra
    • Take the dog for a walk
    • Be kind to that annoying bloke at work
  6. We then try and be mindful of our 3 4 Me intentions during the day.
  7. At bed time we record moments in the day that made us Smile Out Loud or happy moments

    Smile Out Loud suggestions

    • Had a nice time in the sun
    • Watched a silly film
    • Did some exercises
    • I found a pound!
    • An old lady told me a funny joke
    • Had a great sandwich
  8. Then we do a Yoga Nidra exercise and pass out
